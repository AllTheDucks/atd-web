Professional Services

All the Ducks offers a range of professional services to help you meet your LMS usage and development goals.

Blackboard Building Block Development

If you have particular integration requirements, or need custom functionality implemented as a Building Block, we can provide a cost effective service to meet your needs.

Support and Maintenance of open source Building Blocks and extensions.

Need an open source Building Block, but don't have the skills in-house to support it?. We can provide either on-going support for these tools, or time-boxed support around your upgrade window.

Technical Training and mentoring for Blackboard development.

Want to start writing Building Blocks, but don't know where to start? We can provide one-off training for your developers, or an ongoing support service for your technical staff.
